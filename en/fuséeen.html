Here is a motion design that I created as part of a team with other students, using After Effects and Illustrator. Our task was to explain the workings of a startup by creating a video. Thus, we chose to create a motion design to challenge ourselves by incorporating a voiceover. Our process unfolded as follows:

The first step was ideation, followed by the distribution of roles. One group took care of the video script and the voiceover, another group worked on the motion design storyline, and a final group created the visual elements (assets) for the motion.

This project was immensely enriching. It enhanced our ability to effectively collaborate as a team while mastering a complex tool like After Effects. The experience was a valuable opportunity to learn and develop our skills in design and animation.
