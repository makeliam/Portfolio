Selenite: Lost Contact

In *Selenite: Lost Contact*, you play as a spaceship pilot tasked with defending a lunar base that has been overrun by alien forces.

Your mission is to destroy a giant enemy cannon by disabling its energy shield. To do this, you will need to destroy outposts while surviving waves of alien ships.

This game focuses on intense space combat and immerses you in a futuristic universe where humans are fighting to reclaim control of the Moon.


Key features of the game:

  • Dynamic gameplay with enemy waves
  • Visual effects enhanced with post-processing for maximum immersion
  • Captivating storyline set in a near-future scenario
